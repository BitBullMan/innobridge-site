Product Architecture

Five modules, one intelligence layer.

The assistant is not a general chatbot. It is the query interface for Innobridge data, watchlists, regulatory calendar, briefing outputs, and source review workflow.

Sample Output

This week’s stablecoin compliance risk is concentrated around freeze activity, sanctions exposure, and regulatory deadlines.

For a small VASP or payment team, the decision signal is not only whether an address is listed. The more useful question is whether recent freeze events, sanctions references, and upcoming regulatory dates change your onboarding, monitoring, or escalation priorities.

Stablecoin freeze activity remains a primary watch item.
High

Prioritize USDT / USDC address checks where payment flows, OTC counterparties, or customer deposits touch newly frozen or issuer-listed addresses.

Sanctions-related address context needs source-backed review.
Watch

A match should show source, date, jurisdiction, and confidence. A non-match should only be treated as not found in current data, not as proof of safety.

Regulatory deadlines create operational risk.
Info

Compliance teams should map upcoming rules to affected activities such as stablecoin issuance, custody, VASP onboarding, and cross-border transfers.

Informational intelligence only. Innobridge does not provide legal advice, does not determine criminality, and does not replace enterprise transaction monitoring or formal blockchain forensic investigation.
